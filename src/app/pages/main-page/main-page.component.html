Your Valorant Tournament Overlay

Are you currently planning or running a Valorant tournament? Improve your tournaments production quality with Spectra, the live Valorant tournament overlay! Display important information the default Valorant observer experience doesn't conveniently provide, such as player stats, round history, and more! It's easy to use and ready to go at a moment's notice. We can provide you with everything you need to get started right away!

Live Data

Spectra processes events so quickly, the overlay updates before you can even blink.

Free of Charge

We proudly offer Spectra at no cost to you. It's free and open-source!

Single Observer

Only one observer needs to run our observer in addition to their usual setup.

Plug & Play

Once you decide on using Spectra, starting up is easy and fast.

Reliable

We will make sure that there are no sudden outages, so your games will not be interrupted.

Global Availability

With servers in North America and in Europe, we can provide Spectra wherever you are.

Fast Development

Valorant adds a new agent, weapon or map? We'll add support for it as soon as we can. The show must go on!

More in the Future

We are planning to expand Spectra to include more tools for tournament hosting.
